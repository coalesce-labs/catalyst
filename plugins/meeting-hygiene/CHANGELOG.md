### Meeting Hygiene Plugin Launch

Extract meeting workflow skills—agenda prep, structured notes, cleanup automation, and effectiveness feedback—into the new `catalyst-meeting-hygiene` plugin. This focused plugin gives you `/catalyst-meeting-hygiene:meeting-agenda`, `/catalyst-meeting-hygiene:meeting-notes`, and two other specialized skills for end-to-end meeting productivity. These skills moved cleanly from `catalyst-pm` with no behavior changes, just cleaner organization.
