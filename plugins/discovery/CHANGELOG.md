### Catalyst Discovery Plugin

Extract user research, metrics, and prototyping workflows into a focused 14-skill plugin with `/catalyst-discovery:` commands. Includes cohort retention analysis, JTBD interview guides, experiment design trees, and ASCII wireframe generation—all the discovery tools previously scattered in catalyst-pm. This is the first plugin split from the CTL-318 restructure, with independent versioning and marketplace registration.
