INSERT OR IGNORE INTO belief (tick_id, stratum, name, subject, rule_id, source_fact_ids)
SELECT t.tick_id, 2, 'wedged_never_started', s.ticket || '/' || s.phase, 'R4',
       json_array('b' || sr.belief_id, 's' || s.fact_id, 't' || t.tick_id)
FROM tick t
JOIN obs_signal s ON s.tick_id = t.tick_id AND s.status = 'running'
JOIN belief sr    ON sr.tick_id = t.tick_id AND sr.name = 'session_registered'
                 AND sr.subject = s.ticket || '/' || s.phase
JOIN cfg c        ON c.key = 'never_started_ms'
WHERE t.tick_id = :tick
  AND s.started_at_ms IS NOT NULL
  AND t.now_ms - s.started_at_ms > c.value_int
  AND NOT EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM belief b WHERE b.tick_id = t.tick_id
                  AND b.name = 'turn_started' AND b.subject = sr.subject)
  AND NOT EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM belief b WHERE b.tick_id = t.tick_id
                  AND b.name = 'worker_dead' AND b.subject = sr.subject)

About this engine ✕

Click a rule, a chip, or a feed row. Every belief is a sentence — a predicate (the rule's name) said about an entity: a ticket-phase like CTL-722/plan, a whole ticket, or a host. The engine re-derives every belief each 5s tick from observations; what you watch here is the belief base changing.

about
the entity a belief is about — the ground argument of the derived predicate. Three kinds exist: ticket-phase (CTL-722/plan), ticket (CTL-722), host (host:mini).
because
the rule body — the premises that forced the conclusion, positive and negative alike.
¬ checked, absent this tick
stratified negation: the engine looked for the belief in the completed lower stratum and proved it absent. Equal standing with positive premises.
threshold
a live cfg fact (window, cap, cooldown) joined into the rule — shown with its humane form (120,000 ms · 2m).
concludes
the head predicate the rule derives when every premise holds.
derives {…}
the value payload of a conclusion. Some rules derive none — their existence is the verdict (R4, R6).
entered / cleared
the only honest live signal: a (rule, entity) pair appearing in — or dropping out of — the belief base between ticks. Re-derivations are stillness.

Enforcement is DARK: the engine records and reasons, it does not act. R16/R17 predictions never dispatch — see this transition in Journey →
